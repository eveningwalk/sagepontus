Protocol Guide

Mock content
Red Flag

Findings that may require urgent medical referral before continuing routine PT treatment.

  • Cauda equina signs
  • Progressive neurological deficit
  • Major trauma with concerning presentation
Yellow Flag

Findings that support physician checkup, monitoring, documentation, and communication.

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Night pain or systemic symptoms
  • Steroid use, osteoporosis, or complex history
Green Flag

No current escalation trigger identified. Continue PT with routine re-screening.

  • Stable presentation
  • No selected escalation indicators
  • Plan of care and monitoring documented
