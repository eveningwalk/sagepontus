6 Red Flag Criteria

Not AI guesswork — every screen runs the same evidence-based protocol physical therapists learn in school. The same criteria that, when missed, become the basis for malpractice suits.

Source: Goodman C, Snyder T. Differential Diagnosis for Physical Therapists, 5th ed.

  1. Age over 50 or under 20 years
  2. History of cancer / prior malignancy
  3. Unexplained weight loss or systemic signs
  4. Pain unrelieved by rest or position change
  5. Severe, constant, progressive nighttime pain
  6. Failure to improve after 4–6 weeks of PT
